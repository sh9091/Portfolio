History

Pizza originated in Naples, Italy as a quick and easy meal for working-class citizens to eat on the go. Pizza started to gain popularity in North America in the 1940s when Italians started to immigrate and reside there. It is now a trendy dish around the world as it allows for the customization of toppings. Pizza is known for being a popular party dish due to most people enjoying it.
